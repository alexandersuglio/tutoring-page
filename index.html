About 👋

Hello! My name is Alex, and welcome to my tutoring site! Are you struggling with a particular subject in school? Do you feel like you could use some extra help to boost your grades and understanding of the material? Well look no further!

I offer personalized tutoring services to students of all ages and skill levels for math and computer science classes. Whether you are struggling to grasp the basics or need help with advanced topics, I can help you achieve your academic goals!

I have over 10 years of experience working with students at the high school and university level. While attending the University of Florida, I worked extensively with student athletes as a tutor for microeconomics, macroeconomics, and various other math courses. I also have experience as a substitute teacher at Cardinal Gibbons High School before returning to school to obtain my Master's Degree.

I recently graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a Master's in Computer Science. While in school I worked as an engineering intern at NASA Johnson Space Center. At NASA, I worked on the development of cutting-edge software solutions focusing on humans living, working, and thriving in space for the Space Medicine Operations Division.

With my patient and supportive approach, I can guide you through complex concepts and ensure that you have a solid foundation for future learning. Let's get started!
